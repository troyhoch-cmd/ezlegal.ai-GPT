Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal information and legal data with enterprise-grade security and ethical AI practices.

Last Updated: January 15, 2026 | Effective Date: January 15, 2026

Our Commitment: ezLegal.ai™ is dedicated to making legal assistance accessible and affordable for everyone. We protect your privacy with the same care we give to making justice accessible, ensuring your personal and legal information is secure, confidential, and used only to serve you better.

1. Introduction

ezLegal.ai™ ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website and use our services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at https://ezlegal.ai and use our AI-powered legal assistance services.

Who We Serve: ezLegal.ai™ serves individuals, families, small businesses, pro bono organizations, and consumers who need legal assistance but cannot afford traditional lawyer fees. We are NOT a service for lawyers or law firms.

Powered by LegalBreeze: Our platform and AI capabilities are provided by our parent company, LegalBreeze.com (https://legalbreeze.com).

2. Ethical AI Commitment

Our Ethical AI Principles

As an AI-powered service dedicated to access to justice, we commit to the following ethical principles:

  • Fairness: Our AI systems are designed to provide equitable assistance regardless of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, or economic status.
  • Transparency: We are open about when you're interacting with AI versus human support.
  • Privacy-First AI: Your data is NEVER used to train our AI models without explicit consent.
  • Human Oversight: Critical decisions involving your legal matters are subject to human review.
  • Accessibility: We strive to make our AI tools accessible to people with disabilities and those with limited technical skills.
  • No Discrimination: We actively monitor and test our AI systems to prevent algorithmic bias and discrimination.

2.1 AI Training Data

Your Data is Yours: We do NOT use your personal information, case details, documents, or conversations with our ezLegal.ai Chatbot to train or improve our AI models unless you explicitly opt-in to help improve our services. If you choose to help, all data is anonymized and stripped of personally identifiable information.

2.2 AI Transparency

When you interact with our services, we clearly indicate:

  • When you are communicating with an AI system
  • When you are communicating with a human support representative
  • The limitations of AI-generated legal information
  • When you should seek professional legal counsel

2.3 AI Safety and Accuracy

We employ multiple safeguards to ensure our AI provides accurate and safe information:

  • Regular testing and monitoring for accuracy and bias
  • Human oversight of AI-generated legal information
  • Clear disclaimers about the limitations of AI-generated content
  • Continuous improvement based on user feedback and legal expert review

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Create an account (name, email address, password)
  • Use our AI legal assistant (questions, case details, legal issues)
  • Upload documents (legal documents, forms, contracts)
  • Contact us for support (name, email, message content)
  • Subscribe to our services (payment information processed by secure third-party processors)

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent, features used, clickstream data
  • Location Information: General geographic location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

3.3 Sensitive Information

Legal Information Privacy: We understand that information about your legal matters is highly sensitive. We treat all information you share about your legal situation, case details, and related documents with the highest level of confidentiality and security.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

4.1 To Provide Our Services

  • Operate and maintain the ezLegal.ai™ platform
  • Provide AI-powered legal assistance and document generation
  • Manage your account and provide customer support
  • Process payments and fulfill subscriptions
  • Store your documents securely for your future access

4.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Develop new features and services
  • Test and monitor AI system performance and accuracy
  • Conduct research to improve access to justice (only with anonymized data)

4.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send service updates and important notices
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send educational content about legal rights (only if you opt-in)
  • Notify you about changes to our services or policies

4.4 For Security and Legal Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
  • Protect the rights, property, and safety of ezLegal.ai™, our users, and the public
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements

5. How We Share Your Information

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Information

Important: We NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • LegalBreeze.com: Our parent company that provides AI technology and software capabilities
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure servers that store our data (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for processing payments (they receive only payment information)
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics for understanding website usage (anonymized)
  • Email Services: For sending service notifications and support responses

All service providers are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for the specific purposes we authorize.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Law enforcement requests
  • Protection of our legal rights
  • Prevention of fraud or security threats
  • Compliance with regulatory obligations

5.4 Business Transfers

If ezLegal.ai™ is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with other parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

6.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your information
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for security threats and vulnerabilities
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing

6.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Employee training on data privacy and security
  • Background checks for employees with data access
  • Confidentiality agreements with all staff and contractors
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches

6.3 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you promptly as required by law and take immediate steps to mitigate the impact.

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Export: Request a portable copy of your data in a common format
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@ezlegal.ai
  • Or use the account settings in your ezLegal.ai™ dashboard

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7.3 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to delete personal information (with certain exceptions)
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell your information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

7.4 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

8.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (login sessions, security)
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our website (Google Analytics)
  • Marketing Cookies: Track visits across websites for marketing (only with consent)

8.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our website.

8.3 Do Not Track

Some browsers have "Do Not Track" features. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to Do Not Track signals, so our website does not respond to these signals.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Legal Documents: Retained while your account is active or as required by law
  • Usage Data: Typically retained for 12-24 months
  • After Account Closure: We may retain certain information for legal compliance, dispute resolution, and fraud prevention (typically 30 days, up to 7 years for financial records)

10. Children's Privacy

ezLegal.ai™ is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will promptly delete that information. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at privacy@ezlegal.ai.

11. Access to Justice Commitments

Our Access to Justice Mission

As a service dedicated to making legal assistance accessible to all, we make special privacy commitments:

  • Financial Privacy: We will never use your financial hardship status against you or share it with third parties
  • Pro Bono Support: Information about your use of pro bono or sliding-scale services is kept strictly confidential
  • No Credit Checks: We do not perform credit checks or use credit history in determining your eligibility for services
  • Anonymized Research: We may use anonymized, aggregated data to advocate for better access to justice policies
  • Language Access: We provide privacy notices in multiple languages to ensure everyone understands their rights
  • Disability Accommodations: We provide accessible privacy controls for users with disabilities

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

13. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. When we transfer your information internationally, we use appropriate safeguards such as:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Other lawful transfer mechanisms

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for significant changes)
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our website

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
