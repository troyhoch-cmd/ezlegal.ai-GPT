1. Executive Summary

ezLegal.ai is an AI-powered legal information platform designed to democratize access to legal knowledge for consumers and small businesses who cannot afford traditional legal representation.

Platform Capabilities

  • AI Legal Assistant: Jurisdiction-aware legal information chatbot
  • Attorney Matching: Directory of verified lawyers with pro bono options
  • Document Resources: Legal forms, templates, and educational content
  • Crisis Escalation: Immediate resources for emergencies

Key Differentiators

Ethical AI Framework: Comprehensive governance policies compliant with EU AI Act, Colorado AI Act

Access to Justice Mission: Free tier, pro bono pathways, legal aid partnerships

Transparency: Clear disclaimers, source documentation, methodology explanation

Safety-First Design: Crisis detection, human escalation paths, persistent safety resources

3. AI Transparency & Methodology

Architecture: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Our AI uses a 5-step process to generate responses:

StepProcessDescription
1Question AnalysisNLP extracts legal concepts, practice area, entities
2Jurisdiction MatchingUser-selected state prioritized, federal law included
3Source RetrievalSemantic search across curated legal database
4Response GenerationLLM synthesizes with attorney-reviewed prompts
5Safety CheckCrisis detection, UPL guardrails, bias monitoring

Data Sources

SourceDescriptionUpdate Frequency
Federal StatutesU.S. Code, CFR, Federal RegisterWeekly
State StatutesAll 50 states + territoriesMonthly
Case LawFederal and state court decisionsDaily
Agency GuidanceEEOC, FTC, CFPB, DOL publicationsWeekly
Legal Aid ResourcesSelf-help guides nationwideMonthly
Attorney-Reviewed ContentPractice guides and templatesQuarterly

What "Attorney-Reviewed" Means

What It IS:
  • Licensed attorneys design system prompts
  • Document templates reviewed by attorneys
  • Legal team audits AI responses regularly
  • Attorneys identify edge cases for training
What It is NOT:
  • Individual responses are NOT reviewed before delivery
  • AI responses are NOT attorney work product
  • Using ezLegal.ai does NOT create attorney-client relationship
  • Information is NOT protected by privilege

Six Core Governance Principles

  1. Access to Justice Mission: Democratize legal knowledge
  2. Human-Centricity & Oversight: AI assists, humans decide
  3. Transparency & Explainability: Clear about capabilities and limits
  4. Fairness & Bias Mitigation: Regular audits, diverse testing
  5. Robustness & Safety: Continuous monitoring, red teaming
  6. Accountability & Governance: AI Governance Board oversight

4. Safety Features

Crisis Detection System

The platform automatically detects crisis situations and surfaces immediate resources:

Crisis TypeTrigger ExamplesPrimary ResourcePriority
Domestic Violence "abusive relationship", "restraining order" National DV Hotline: 1-800-799-7233 HIGH
Self-Harm "suicide", "want to die" 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline HIGH
Homelessness "evicted", "homeless" HUD Housing: 1-800-569-4287 HIGH
Detention "arrested", "detained" ACLU Know Your Rights HIGH
Urgent Deadline "court tomorrow", "filing deadline" Attorney referral prioritized MEDIUM

Safety Checkpoint Flow

Before first chat interaction, users must complete:

  1. Acknowledge AI limitations disclaimer
  2. Select their jurisdiction (state)
  3. Confirm understanding that this is information, not legal advice
  4. Acknowledge crisis resources are available

Persistent Safety Elements

Always Visible in Chat Interface:
  • Crisis Help button (direct link to 988)
  • Free Legal Aid link (pro bono eligibility)
  • Find Lawyer link (attorney directory)
  • How AI Works link (transparency)
  • Disclaimer banner ("AI provides information, not legal advice")

When AI Recommends Human Attorney

The system explicitly recommends attorney consultation for:

  • Court appearances
  • Complex transactions
  • Criminal charges
  • Custody disputes
  • Immigration matters
  • Personal injury claims
  • Estate planning
  • Business formation

5. Accessibility Compliance

WCAG 2.1 AA Standards

Perceivable

  • Text alternatives for all images (alt text)
  • Sufficient color contrast (4.5:1 minimum)
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • No content relies solely on color

Operable

  • Full keyboard navigation
  • Skip links for main content
  • Visible focus indicators
  • No keyboard traps

Understandable

  • Consistent navigation patterns
  • Form input labels and instructions
  • Error identification and suggestions
  • Plain language where possible

Robust

  • Valid HTML markup
  • ARIA attributes for dynamic content
  • Assistive technology compatibility

Specific Implementations

Icon Accessibilityaria-hidden="true" on decorative icons
Interactive Labelsaria-label on all buttons and links

6. Security Architecture

Data Protection Layers

LayerImplementationStandard
Transit EncryptionTLS 1.3Industry Standard
At-Rest EncryptionAES-256Industry Standard
Database SecurityRow-Level Security (RLS)Supabase
AuthenticationSecure sessions, JWTSupabase Auth
API SecurityRate limiting, input validationOWASP

Row-Level Security Policies

  • Users can only access their own data
  • Authenticated users required for sensitive operations
  • Admin functions require is_admin flag verification
  • No public access to user data by default

Compliance Certifications

  • SOC 2 Type II: Controls implemented, audit available
  • CCPA: California consumer data rights honored
  • HIPAA-Ready: Architecture supports healthcare data if needed
  • GDPR-Compatible: Data handling supports EU rights
